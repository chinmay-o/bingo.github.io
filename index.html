How to Play Bingo
  1. Click the column of your choice to add numbers till 25 in ascending order.
  2. After filling all 25 column you can call a number between 1 and 25 and touch it to mark that number and pass the chance to next player.
  3. The number you called should be marked by all your opponents and vise versa.
  4. For every vertical, horizontal and diagonal marked coloumn a letter from BINGO will be crossed.
  5. The first one to cross all 5 letters will be the winner.
